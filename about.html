The First Einasvel

The original idea came to me slowly. I've always liked the idea of strange blue humanoids with unconventional mannerisms, and I finally found the opportunity to bring them to life as part of an assignment for an art class two years ago.


Inspired by various owl species, vultures, other primate species, prehistoric creatures, 1920's to 1930's traditional Rwandan hairstyles, and various black subcultures


Funny story, because I'm completely terrible at naming things, I was stuck on what to call this species at first. I eventually spotted a thing of Vaseline on my desk and resorted to rearranging the letters until I came up with Einasvel. After later creating the language and sounds, I translated it into the closest thing, Enausq'/hl/, which is why you see those words used interchangeably throughout this site!

Humanized Einasvel/Oele'in

Eleanor and Maukkohd (above) are the humanized versions of the Oele'in E/hl/aunoh and the Enausq'/hl/ Maukkohd. I wanted to play with how they would be on Earth while keeping as many of their original features as possible. With Maukkohd, I kept her height, body type, shape of the hairstyle, large eyes, small eyebrows, and large lips. I added strong cheekbones to outline the face similarly to how the fur does, and added the facial hair/bangs to do the same. Eleanor is translated much more leniently, as I mainly focused on making her an outdoorsy and adventurous character. She spends most of her time outside climbing trees and exploring the forest outside their house.
